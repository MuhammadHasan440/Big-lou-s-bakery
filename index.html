Our Story

Baking is an
Act of Love

Big Lou started in a tiny kitchen in East Austin with nothing but a borrowed mixer and a belief that the best bread is made after midnight. Today, we're still hand-mixing every dough, still firing the oven at 4 AM, and still believing that a warm cookie can make any night better.

We source local Texas wheat, farm-fresh dairy, and seasonal produce from Hill Country growers. No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just honest ingredients and time-honored techniques.
